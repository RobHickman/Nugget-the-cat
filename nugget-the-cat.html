My Cat Nugget

Written in dedication to my old cat nugget

I had a cat named nugget for about 6 years. He was my favorite cat. He was quirky and loved to cuddle

Here are some of his quirks

  • He loved cheese. whether is was cheesy chips are or fresh off of a block, he would try and eat it all.
  • Lazer pointers made him an olymic athlete. he could run around the house endlessly.
  • He loved to cuddle on fuzzy blankets. When he plopped down, he would be purring immediately.

As for his looks, he was a portly kitty. He was about 13lb of fluffy spunk. He was a mixture of black, brown and gray.
